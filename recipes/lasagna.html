Lasagna

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2 cans (15 ounces each) tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for a bit of heat)
  • 9 lasagna noodles, cooked al dente
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet or saucepan, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add chopped onions and minced garlic, cooking until softened.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, oregano, basil, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes. Simmer for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  5. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese with beaten egg. Mix well.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a greased 9x13-inch baking dish
  7. Place three lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  8. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a third of the mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  9. Repeat the layers, ending with a final layer of meat sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  10. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  12. Allow the lasagna to rest for about 15 minutes before slicing. This helps the layers set.
  13. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley before serving.
